# Skill: data/data-storytelling

## Purpose
Produce a Data Narrative — a human-readable, audience-specific explanation of what the data means, written in plain language for a non-technical audience. Translates compliance scores, entity counts, and finding patterns into a story that drives action. Used in board packs, customer-facing summaries, and stakeholder briefings.

## Inputs
- `artifacts/data/analytics-requirements.md`
- `artifacts/ideate/personas/{audience-slug}.md`
- `artifacts/data/reports/` (any relevant report specs)
- `artifacts/strategy/okrs.md`
- **Argument required:** audience slug (e.g. `board-executive`, `compliance-officer`, `customer-success`)

## Output
**File:** `artifacts/data/narratives/{audience-slug}.md`
**Registers in manifest:** yes

## Narrative Rules (enforced)
- No technical jargon. Assume zero knowledge of the product's internals.
- Every number tells a story — what does it mean? Is it good or bad? What should happen next?
- Narrative is actionable: every section ends with a "So what?" and a recommended action.
- Privacy: narratives never include entity values. Aggregate counts and patterns only.
- Narrative templates are reusable — they parameterise with real data at generation time.

## Narrative: Board / Executive Summary

**Reading time:** 3 minutes
**Purpose:** Give leadership a clear picture of the organisation's data compliance health — no jargon, clear actions.

---

### What We Measured

Between {period_start} and {period_end}, the {product_name} system scanned **{files_scanned:,} files** across **{locations_count} storage locations**, covering **{coverage_pct}%** of your registered data estate.

---

### Where You Stand

Your overall compliance posture across **{frameworks_active} active frameworks** is currently:

> **{posture_score}%** — {posture_label: Excellent | Good | Needs Attention | At Risk}

{posture_score >= 90}: Your data estate is in excellent compliance health. The few open findings should be reviewed in your next compliance cycle.

{posture_score 70–89}: Your compliance posture is solid, with room for improvement. Focus on the {critical_count} critical and {high_count} high-severity findings — these represent your most significant compliance risk.

{posture_score < 70}: Your compliance posture requires immediate attention. There are {critical_count} critical findings that expose the organisation to regulatory risk. See Section 3 for the recommended action plan.

---

### What We Found

During this period, the system identified **{entity_types_found}** categories of sensitive information across your estate:

| What was found | Where it appeared | Compliance implication |
|---------------|------------------|----------------------|
| Personal names and email addresses | {top_location_1}, {top_location_2} | Subject to GDPR right-to-access and erasure requirements |
| Financial account numbers | {top_location_3} | In scope for PCI-DSS controls on data storage |
| {entity_type_3} | {top_location_4} | {framework_implication_3} |

**Important:** We found sensitive data where it wasn't expected to be. This is common — and finding it is the first step to fixing it.

---

### Your Biggest Risks

Based on the findings, your top 3 compliance risks are:

**1. {top_risk_1_rule} — {severity_1}**
> {plain-language explanation of what the rule is and why it matters}
> **Action required:** {specific, concrete action} by {recommended deadline}

**2. {top_risk_2_rule} — {severity_2}**
> {plain-language explanation}
> **Action required:** {action}

**3. {top_risk_3_rule} — {severity_3}**
> {plain-language explanation}
> **Action required:** {action}

---

### Progress Since Last Period

{delta_positive}: Your compliance posture improved by **{delta}%** since {prior_period}. This reflects {what_changed — e.g. "the resolution of 8 findings in your HR systems"}.

{delta_negative}: Your compliance posture decreased by **{abs(delta)}%** since {prior_period}. This is primarily due to {what_changed — e.g. "the discovery of 12 new findings in recently connected storage locations"}.

{delta_neutral}: Your compliance posture is stable compared to {prior_period}.

---

### Recommended Next Steps

1. **Immediate (this week):** Review and assign the {critical_count} critical findings to a named owner. Deadline: {7_days_from_now}
2. **Short-term (30 days):** Resolve high-severity findings in {top_location_1} and {top_location_2}
3. **Ongoing:** Schedule quarterly scans to detect new data as your estate grows

*This report was generated by {product_name} on {generated_at}. Data covers the period {period_start} to {period_end}. For technical detail or to export this report, contact your Compliance Officer.*

## Quality Checks
- [ ] No technical jargon in any audience-facing section
- [ ] Every section ends with a "So what?" and a recommended action
- [ ] All numbers include their units and context (not just raw figures)
- [ ] Privacy: no entity values — only types, counts, and location names
- [ ] Template uses {placeholder} syntax for parameterisation at generation time
- [ ] Board narrative is 3 minutes reading time or less
- [ ] Compliance officer narrative connects findings to specific OKRs
